## Conclusion

All statistical tests produced extremely small p-values, providing strong evidence against the null hypotheses. The analyses confirm that:

• High-rated and low-rated movies have significantly different mean ratings.
• The distribution of ratings differs significantly between groups.
• Movie genres significantly influence user ratings.
